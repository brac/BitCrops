Pitch:
Stardew Valley clone played in a browser.

Tech:
Javescript
Phaser

Imagery:
2D, top down bitmapped based

Basic Gameplay:
Moving PC to different points and interacting. For example:
Move to field, plant seends
Move to town, converse with NPCs
Move to store, buy items
Move to home, sleep
Move to field, harvest
Move to seller, profit
